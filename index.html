About Me

I'm Kassi Montenegro, a career consultant dedicated to helping professionals navigate critical transitions and build meaningful careers.

With experience spanning higher education, technology, and law, I understand the unique challenges and opportunities in each field. My approach is deeply personal—I don't believe in one-size-fits-all solutions. Instead, I work with you to uncover your values, strengths, and authentic career aspirations.

Whether you're a student exploring academic paths, a career-changer entering tech, or a prospective law student, my goal is to empower you with the clarity and confidence to make decisions that align with who you are and who you want to become.

Clarity

Clear thinking about what matters most in your career

Direction

Strategic guidance tailored to your unique situation

Confidence

The assurance to take decisive action toward your goals
